# Using Your AI Workshop Facilitator

## 🤖 About This AI Assistant

Welcome! You're using an **AI-powered workshop facilitator** designed to help guide you through AI Discovery Workshops. This page explains what this AI can and cannot do, helping you get the most out of your experience.

---

## What is This?

This is an artificial intelligence system that:
- Uses large language models (LLMs) from Azure OpenAI
- Has been trained on workshop facilitation methodologies
- Provides guidance through specialized AI "agents" with different expertise
- Learns from conversations to maintain context during your session

**Important:** This is an AI assistant, not a human facilitator. Responses are generated by AI and should be verified for your specific context.

## ⚠️ Important Limitations to Know

### AI Hallucinations
**What it means:** AI may sometimes generate confident but incorrect information.

**Why it happens:** The AI generates responses based on patterns in training data, not verified facts.

**What to do:**
- ✅ Verify important details against authoritative sources
- ✅ Cross-check statistics or specific claims
- ✅ Use AI guidance as a starting point, not final authority
- ✅ Consult subject matter experts for critical decisions

### No Company-Specific Knowledge
**What it means:** The AI doesn't know your company's specific processes, culture, or constraints.

**Why it happens:** The AI has no access to your internal systems or documents.

**What to do:**
- ✅ Provide relevant context in your questions
- ✅ Adapt AI suggestions to your company's reality
- ✅ Verify feasibility with your IT and business teams
- ✅ Consider company policies when implementing suggestions

### Training Data Cutoff
**What it means:** The AI's knowledge has a cutoff date and may not include recent developments.

**Why it happens:** AI models are trained on data up to a specific date.

**What to do:**
- ✅ Check for recent changes in technology or methodologies
- ✅ Verify current best practices
- ✅ Supplement with up-to-date resources
- ✅ Ask your facilitator about recent developments

### Bias Potential
**What it means:** AI responses may reflect biases present in training data.

**Why it happens:** Training data can contain societal biases or over-represent certain perspectives.

**What to do:**
- ✅ Consider diverse perspectives beyond AI suggestions
- ✅ Question assumptions in AI responses
- ✅ Report concerning patterns or biases
- ✅ Bring in diverse human expertise

## 🛡️ Privacy & Your Data

### What We Collect
- Your conversation messages (to maintain context during your session)
- Usage analytics (anonymized - which features you use, response times)
- Feedback ratings (thumbs up/down on responses)
- Basic account information (username, email for authentication)

### How We Use Your Data
- **Provide Service:** Maintain conversation context, improve your experience
- **Improve Quality:** Analyze feedback to enhance the AI assistant
- **Monitor Safety:** Detect abuse or concerning patterns
- **Support:** Help troubleshoot issues if you contact us

### How We Protect Your Data
- 🔒 **Encrypted:** Your data is encrypted both in transit (HTTPS/TLS 1.2+) and at rest (Azure Storage encryption)
- 🚫 **Not for Training:** Your conversations are NOT used to train AI models (Azure OpenAI guarantee)
- 👤 **Isolated:** Your conversations are separate from other users; no cross-user access
- ⏱️ **Auto-Deleted:** Conversations automatically deleted after 90 days of inactivity
- 🌍 **Secure Storage:** Data stored in Microsoft Azure with enterprise security controls

### Your Data Rights
- **Export:** Request a copy of your conversation history
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your data at any time
- **Review:** View your conversation history within the app

**To exercise these rights:** Contact privacy@your-org.com

### What NOT to Share
**For your privacy, please DO NOT share:**
- ❌ Social Security numbers, credit card numbers, or other sensitive IDs
- ❌ Passwords or authentication credentials
- ❌ Confidential business information or trade secrets
- ❌ Personal health information
- ❌ Information about other people without their consent

## 🔍 Understanding AI Behavior

### Why Responses Vary
The same question might get slightly different answers each time because:
- AI uses "temperature" settings for creativity vs. consistency
- Responses build on conversation context
- AI generates probabilistic responses, not database lookups

This is normal and expected!

### Why AI Sometimes Refuses Requests
The AI may decline certain requests to:
- Stay focused on workshop facilitation (its primary purpose)
- Maintain safety and security guardrails
- Avoid providing advice outside its expertise
- Protect privacy and prevent misuse

This is by design and protects everyone.

### How Agent Switching Works
- **Multi-Agent System:** You may interact with different AI "experts"
- **Automatic Routing:** The system intelligently routes you to the right expert
- **Specialized Knowledge:** Each agent has focused expertise
- **Seamless Transitions:** You'll be notified when switching agents

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### "Is this a human or AI?"
**100% AI.** All responses are generated by artificial intelligence. A human facilitator should always be involved in actual workshops.

### "Can the AI see what other people ask?"
**No.** Your conversations are completely separate from other users. The AI cannot access other users' sessions.

### "Does my company/Microsoft train AI on my conversations?"
**No.** Your conversations are NOT used for AI model training. This is guaranteed by Azure OpenAI.

### "How long is my data kept?"
**90 days.** Conversations are automatically deleted after 90 days of inactivity. You can request deletion sooner.

### "What if the AI gives wrong information?"
**Verify it.** Always cross-check important information with authoritative sources or human experts. If you notice errors, please report them.

### "Can I trust the AI's suggestions?"
**Use as guidance, not gospel.** The AI provides helpful starting points, but human judgment is essential, especially for your specific context.

### "Is my data secure?"
**Yes.** We use enterprise-grade security including encryption, private endpoints, and Microsoft Azure's security infrastructure.

### "What if I need help right now?"
**Contact your facilitator** or email support@your-org.com for immediate assistance.
